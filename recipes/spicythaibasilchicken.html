Thai Basil Chicken Recipe

Description

Thai basil chicken is a classic stir-fry that is popular in Thai cuisine.
It is cooked in flavorful spices and savory seasonings.
The unique topping is fresh basil which transforms this dish after cooking.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbs oyster sauce
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 lb ground chicken thighs
  • ¼ cup sliced shallots
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbs minced Thai chilies or any chillies
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cups steamed rice

Steps

  1. Mix chicken broth, oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, white sugar and brown sugar in a bowl.

  2. Heat pan or wok over high heat and add oil. Add shallots, garlic, and sliced chilies to carmelize.

  3. Add chicken and stir until brown.

  4. Add sauce mixture and stir until sauce begins to caramelize.

  5. Cook and stir to deglaze the sauce. Remove from heat after the meat is glazed.

  6. Stir in basil until wilted. Serve over steamed rice and enjoy.
