Pho Recipe

Description

The pan-seared ribeye steak is the ultimate meat-lover's dish.
Cooked with minimal seasoning and on its own is a hearty meal that will satisfy anyone.

Ingredients

  • ribeye steak
  • salt
  • ground black-pepper
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 4 tbs unsalted butter
  • 4 thyme sprigs
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 rosemary sprig

Steps

  1. Season the ribeye steak with salt and ground pepper on both sides.

  2. Place a pan on the stove with high heat and add olive oil.

  3. After about 2 minutes, place steak and garlic onto pan and sear for 6 minutes.

  4. Flip, then add butter and thyme. Continue to sear for another 6 minutes.
    Baste butter onto steak repeatedly while searing.

  5. After searing, render fat caps and turn off heat.
    Remove steak from pan.

  6. Let steak rest for 5 minutes.
    Slice steak and enjoy.
