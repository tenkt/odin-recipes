Pho Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for Vietnamese Pho beef noodle soup. It is a very savory and heart-warming dish.
The aroma of the broth and the lightness of the soup makes you crave for more with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 3-inch (7 to 8 cm) pieces ginger, cut in half lengthwise (no need to peel)
  • 2 onions (peeled)
  • 5 pounds beef marrow or knuckle bones
  • 2 pounds beef chuck (cut into 2 pieces)
  • 5 quarts water
  • 2 scallions (cut into 4-inch lengths)
  • 1/3 cup fish sauce
  • 2 Â½ ounces rock sugar (2 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar)
  • 8 star anise
  • 6 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 black cardamom pod (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons fennel seeds
  • 2 teaspoons coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 pound dried pho noodles
  • 1/3 pound beef sirloin (slightly frozen, then sliced paper-thin against the grain)

    • Garnishes

  • Sliced chili
  • Thinly sliced onion
  • Chopped scallions
  • Cilantro
  • Mung bean sprouts
  • Thai basil
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Place bones and beef chuck in a large pot and add water to cover it. Boil for 5 minutes. Drain and clean pot.

  2. Char ginger and onions on a stove and rinse charred spots.

  3. Add water 5 quarts of water in the pot and put the beef and bones back in.
    Add charred ginger and onions. Add scallions, fish-sauce and sugar.
    Bring to a boil, then simmer. Skim and remove any foam and fat.

  4. After 40 minutes, remove one piece of chuck and put in a bowl of ice water. Refrigerate to serve later.

  5. Toast spices: star anise, cloves, cinnamon stick, cardamom, fennel seeds, and coriander seeds.
    Use a dry pan over low-medium heat for 3 minutes until fragrant.
    Put spices inside a cheese-cloth, tie with kitchen-string and add to broth.

  6. Cover pot and continue simmering for 4 hours. Skim broth and season to taste.

  7. Boil noodles and add to a bowl. Place slices of beef chuck and raw sirloin on top of noodles.
    Bring the broth to a boil and ladle it onto the bowl. It will also cook the raw beef.
    Garnish with toppings and squeeze a slice of lime. Serve and enjoy.
